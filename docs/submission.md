# AgentOps 360 — Hackathon Submission

## Project Title

AgentOps 360 — Autonomous Enterprise Operations Agent

---

## Short Description

AgentOps 360 turns meetings, documents, and audio transcripts into decisions, tasks, risks, follow-up emails, and executive reports using a multi-agent workflow powered by Gemini, Speechmatics, React, ASP.NET Core, and SQL Server.

---

## Long Description

Enterprise teams often lose time after meetings because decisions, tasks, owners, and risks are scattered across notes, documents, recordings, and follow-up messages.

AgentOps 360 solves this by transforming unstructured meeting input into a structured execution dashboard.

Users can paste meeting notes, upload text files, upload PDFs, or upload audio recordings. The system extracts relevant content, runs a multi-agent AI workflow, and generates:

- Executive summary
- Decisions
- Action plan
- Owners
- Priorities
- Risks
- Mitigations
- Follow-up email
- Confidence score
- Agent trace
- Exportable executive report

The app stores every analysis in SQL Server, allowing teams to reopen previous runs and maintain an auditable execution history.

## Business Value

AgentOps 360 creates measurable enterprise value by reducing the time between meetings and execution.

Benefits:

- Faster meeting follow-up
- Clearer task ownership
- Better risk visibility
- Reduced manual reporting
- Improved manager productivity
- Auditable decision history

## Why It Is Original

Most AI assistants stop at summarization. AgentOps 360 goes further by converting meeting content into a full execution workflow with tasks, risks, emails, audit traces, history, and exportable reports.
